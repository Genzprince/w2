# How to Add a Video Project 🎬

This guide explains the simplified data structure and provides instructions on how to add a new project to your high-end portfolio showcase.

---

## 🛠️ Accessing the Portfolio Manager (Admin Panel)

1. Navigate to the **Portfolio Showcase** section on your live page.
2. Click the small **Lock Icon** located in the top-left area of the "Featured Projects" section header.
3. Enter your admin passcode: `prince2026`
4. Click **Unlock** to enter Editor Mode. You can now pin/unpin videos to your **Recent Work** showcase in real-time.
5. Click the **PORTFOLIO MANAGER ⚙️** button that appears to open your full dashboard, where you can add, edit, or reorder any video project.

---

## 📝 Required Fields to Fill

When you click **"Add Video"** in your Portfolio Manager, you will fill out the following simplified, client-focused properties:

### 1. Project Title (`title`) *
* **What it is:** The title of the showcase video.
* **Example:** `We Built a Real-Life Iron Man Suit`

### 2. Creator / Client Name (`client`) *
* **What it is:** The client, brand, or channel name.
* **Example:** `Hacksmith Industries`

### 3. Core Format Category (`category`) *
* **What it is:** The format category dropdown.
* **Options:** `YouTube` | `Gaming` | `Documentary` | `Commercial` | `Motion Graphics` | `Short Form` | `Long Form`

### 4. Platform (`platform`) *
* **What it is:** The platform where the video is primarily showcased.
* **Example:** `YouTube Shorts` or `Instagram Reels`

### 5. Video Duration (`duration`) *
* **What it is:** The duration in `MM:SS` format.
* **Important:** If duration starts with `00:` (e.g., `00:45`), the system automatically registers and styles the video inside the **Short Form** grid.
* **Example:** `00:52` (Shorts) or `11:45` (Long Form)

### 6. Completion Year (`year`) *
* **What it is:** The year of release.
* **Example:** `2024`

### 7. Video URL (`youtubeLink` or `videoUrl`) *
* **What it is:** The direct URL of the video. Supports YouTube, YouTube Shorts, Vimeo, or a direct link to an `.mp4` file.
* **Pro-Tip:** Simply paste any YouTube or Shorts URL, and the system will automatically parse and generate the embed frame seamlessly!

### 8. Cover Thumbnail Artwork (`thumbnail`) *
* **What it is:** Image URL or custom file upload.
* **YouTube Shortcut:** Paste your YouTube video URL, then click the **"Auto Extract YouTube Cover"** button. The system will automatically fetch and set the high-definition cover artwork instantly!

### 9. Project Description (`description`) *
* **What it is:** A compelling, retentive summary of the concept and video style.
* **Example:** `An adrenaline-fueled, high-concept visual showcase detail-engineered to keep viewers hooked from the opening sequence.`

### 10. Role / Title Credit (`role`) *
* **What it is:** Your specific contribution.
* **Example:** `Lead Video Editor & Sound Designer`

### 11. Strong Metric Result (`result`) *
* **What it is:** Exactly **one** strong numerical or retention metric.
* **Example:** `40% Higher Retention` or `5M+ Views` or `120 Videos Edited`

### 12. Narrative Challenge (`challenge`) *
* **What it is:** The creative or pacing hurdle you faced.
* **Example:** `Transforming 40+ hours of raw prototype footage into a tight, punchy narrative without losing the technical context.`

### 13. Retentive Solution (`solution`) *
* **What it is:** How you solved it using editing techniques.
* **Example:** `Designed custom sound effects, integrated split-second speed ramps, and incorporated kinetic text to guide viewer attention through intricate moments.`

### 14. Skills & Keywords (`skills`) *
* **What it is:** Keywords used for tags (comma-separated).
* **Example:** `Sound Design, Kinetic Typography, Speed Ramps`

---

## 💡 Quick Tips for High-Retention Showcase Copy

* **Highlight the Hook:** When writing your **Solution**, emphasize the hook mechanics and visual storytelling triggers you designed. Clients love to see the *strategy* behind your cuts.
* **One Strong Metric:** Keep the **Result** field restricted to a single strong, highly impressive stat. Bold metrics build immediate trust.
